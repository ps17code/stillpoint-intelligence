## Data Confidence Summary

**HIGH confidence (>80% of claims):** Corporate financial data from audited filings, vessel specifications, government program allocations, operational cable system metrics, and well-documented events (Baltic incidents, ASN nationalization).

**MEDIUM confidence (15% of claims):** Market size projections, market share estimates (particularly for private companies like SubCom), demand forecasts, and pricing data from broker reports rather than audited sources.

**LOW-MEDIUM confidence (<5% of claims):** Military fiber demand share (single-source estimate), total manufacturing capacity (industry estimate, not independently verified), and technology deployment timelines (subject to engineering and commercial uncertainty).

## Key Data Gaps

1. **SubCom financial data:** Private company — no audited financials available. Revenue, margin, and backlog figures are industry estimates.
2. **Vessel day rates:** Reported as ranges from broker sources; actual contracted rates are commercially confidential.
3. **HMN Technologies financials:** Limited public disclosure from Hengtong Group subsidiary.
4. **Repeater pricing:** Highly variable by specification and system; benchmark ranges rather than exact figures.
5. **Insurance cost increases:** Vary significantly by policy type, route, and insurer; reported as indicative range.

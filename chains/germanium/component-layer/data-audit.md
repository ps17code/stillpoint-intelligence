# Data Audit — GeO₂ / GeCl₄ Component Layer Insights

## Methodology

Each data point is assigned a confidence level based on source quality and cross-referencing:

- **HIGH** — Government source (USGS, MOFCOM), company filing (annual report, earnings release), or peer-reviewed study. Cross-referenced against at least 2 independent sources.
- **MEDIUM** — Reputable industry analyst report (CRU, Fastmarkets, RFC Ambrian), credible trade publication (Light Reading, Cabling Installation & Maintenance), or company press release. Single-source but from credible origin.
- **LOW** — Market research firm estimate (Grand View, Wiseguy Reports), analyst commentary without primary data, or inference from adjacent data points. Treat as directional, not precise.
- **INFERRED** — Calculated or estimated by the author from multiple data points. Methodology noted.

## Overall confidence assessment

**Strongest data:** Company financials, USGS production figures, MOFCOM policy details, commodity pricing
**Moderate confidence:** Market share estimates, capacity figures, technology performance claims
**Weakest data:** GeCl₄-specific market sizing, military demand quantification, future capacity projections, private company financials

**Key gaps identified:**
1. Umicore does not publish GeCl₄-specific production volumes or revenue
2. No publicly available data on Corning's GeCl₄ consumption volume
3. YOFC's financial specifics are limited in English-language sources
4. Military fiber demand (10.5% claim) needs additional verification
5. GeCl₄ market sizing ($92M) relies on a single market research firm
